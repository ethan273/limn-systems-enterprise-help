# Product Management

Manage product collections, materials, SKUs, and inventory across your catalog.

## Overview

The product management system provides comprehensive tools for organizing products into collections, managing materials and their properties, tracking concepts and prototypes, and maintaining your product catalog.

## Key Modules

- **Collections** - Organize products into collections
- **Materials** - Material library and specifications
- **Material Properties** - Physical and technical properties
- **Concepts** - Product concepts and development
- **Prototypes** - Prototype tracking and testing
- **Product Catalog** - Complete product catalog
- **SKU Management** - Stock keeping unit management
- **Specifications** - Product specifications and details
- **Inventory** - Inventory tracking and management

## Key Features

- Material database with properties
- Collection-based organization
- SKU generation and management
- Product specifications
- Prototype tracking
- Inventory management
- Integration with orders and production
