## Popular Questions

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

### How do I access the portal?
You'll receive an email invitation with a magic link to access your portal.

### What browsers are supported?
We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

### How do I upload documents?
Navigate to the Documents section and click "Upload" or drag and drop files.

### Who do I contact for support?
Contact your system administrator or submit a support ticket.
