# Partner Management

Manage relationships with designers, factories, sourcing agents, and other partners.

## Overview

The partner management system provides tools for collaborating with external partners, tracking performance, managing contracts, and providing portal access.

## Partner Types

- **Designers** - External design partners and freelancers
- **Factories** - Manufacturing partners and suppliers
- **Sourcing Agents** - Procurement and sourcing partners
- **Other Partners** - Logistics, consultants, and service providers

## Key Features

- Partner profiles and contact management
- Performance tracking and metrics
- Contract management and renewals
- Portal access for collaboration
- Document sharing and collaboration
- Payment and invoice tracking
