# Integrations

Third-party integrations and services.

## Overview

Limn Systems Enterprise integrates with leading third-party services for authentication, email, payments, logistics, and more.

## Available Integrations

### Infrastructure & Backend
- **Supabase** - Database, authentication, and storage
- **Vercel** - Hosting and deployment platform
- **Upstash Redis** - Caching and rate limiting

### Communication
- **Resend** - Email delivery service
- **Google Workspace** - SSO and email integration

### Business Operations
- **QuickBooks** - Accounting and financial data sync
- **PandaDoc** - Document generation and e-signatures
- **SEKO Logistics** - Shipping and tracking integration

### Monitoring & Analytics
- **Sentry** - Error tracking and performance monitoring

## Key Features

- OAuth and SSO authentication
- Real-time data synchronization
- Webhook support
- API key management
- Integration health monitoring
- Error handling and retry logic
