# Introduction

Welcome to the Limn Systems Enterprise platform - your complete solution for furniture design, production, and project management.

## What is Limn Systems Enterprise?

Limn Systems Enterprise is a comprehensive platform that brings together:

- **Design Management** - Collaborate on design projects and approvals
- **Product Catalog** - Manage collections, materials, and SKUs
- **Order Management** - Create and track orders through production
- **Production Tracking** - Monitor manufacturing progress in real-time
- **Quality Control** - Ensure product quality with inspection workflows
- **Portal Access** - Dedicated portals for customers, designers, and factories
- **Automation** - Streamline workflows with smart automation

## Who Uses Limn Systems?

The platform serves multiple user types:

- **Internal Teams** - Employees with role-based access
- **Customers** - Order tracking and project visibility
- **Designers** - Project collaboration and deliverable submission
- **Factory Partners** - Production management and quality tracking
- **QC Teams** - Quality inspection and reporting
