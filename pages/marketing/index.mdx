# Marketing Tools

Email campaigns, flipbooks, SMS campaigns, and marketing analytics.

## Overview

The marketing system provides powerful tools for creating and managing email campaigns, interactive flipbooks, SMS campaigns, and tracking marketing performance.

## Key Modules

### Email Marketing
- **Email Campaigns** - Create and manage campaigns
- **Email Templates** - Reusable email templates
- **Email Scheduling** - Schedule campaign delivery
- **Email Analytics** - Track opens, clicks, and engagement
- **Unsubscribe System** - CAN-SPAM compliant unsubscribe

### Flipbooks
- **Flipbooks** - Interactive digital catalogs
- **Flipbook Builder** - Visual flipbook creator
- **Flipbook Hotspots** - Interactive clickable areas
- **Flipbook Analytics** - Track flipbook engagement

### SMS Marketing
- **SMS Campaigns** - Text message campaigns
- **SMS Templates** - Reusable SMS templates

## Key Features

- Email campaign management
- Interactive flipbook creator
- SMS campaign tools
- Marketing analytics
- Template library
- Unsubscribe management
- Resend integration
