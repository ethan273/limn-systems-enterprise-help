# Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Manage leads, contacts, customers, and deals throughout the sales lifecycle.

## Overview

The CRM system helps you track and manage customer relationships from initial lead capture through ongoing customer management. Track interactions, manage deals, and provide excellent customer service.

## Key Modules

- **Leads** - Capture and qualify potential customers
- **Contacts** - Manage individual contact information
- **Customers** - Track customer accounts and history
- **Deals** - Manage sales opportunities and pipeline
- **Communication** - Email and messaging integration
- **Customer Portal** - Self-service portal for customers

## Key Features

- Lead scoring and qualification
- Deal pipeline management
- Contact and account management
- Communication tracking
- Customer portal access
- Integration with order management
