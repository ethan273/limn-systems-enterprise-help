# Financial Management

Manage invoices, payments, expenses, and financial reporting.

## Overview

The financial management system provides comprehensive tools for invoicing, payment tracking, expense management, and financial reporting. Integrates with QuickBooks and PandaDoc.

## Key Modules

- **Invoices** - Customer invoicing
- **Production Invoices** - Factory and supplier invoices
- **Payments** - Payment tracking and recording
- **Payment Allocation** - Allocate payments to invoices
- **Expenses** - Expense tracking and categorization
- **AR Aging** - Accounts receivable aging reports
- **Financial Reports** - Comprehensive financial reporting
- **QuickBooks** - QuickBooks integration setup
- **QuickBooks Sync** - Sync data with QuickBooks
- **PandaDoc** - PandaDoc integration for proposals

## Key Features
