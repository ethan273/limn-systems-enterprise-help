# Order Management

Create and manage customer orders from quote to delivery.

## Overview

The order management system provides end-to-end tracking of customer orders, including items, materials, approvals, and timelines. Orders can be linked to projects and tracked through production and shipping.

## Key Modules

- **Order Creation** - Create new customer orders
- **Order Items** - Manage line items and products
- **Order Materials** - Track materials for each order
- **Order Tracking** - Monitor order progress
- **Order Approval** - Approval workflows
- **Order Timeline** - Timeline and milestone tracking
- **Project Linking** - Link orders to projects

## Key Features

- Multi-item orders
- Material tracking per order
- Approval workflows
- Timeline and milestones
- Project integration
- Production handoff
- Shipping coordination
