# Sales Guide

Complete guide for sales team using Limn Systems Enterprise.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the System
1. Navigate to app.limn.us.com
2. Click "Sign in with Google"
3. Select your @limn.us.com account
4. Access your Sales Dashboard

### Your Role
As a sales team member, you can:
- Manage customer relationships (CRM)
- Create quotes and orders
- Track sales pipeline
- Access sales analytics
- Manage tasks and follow-ups

## Sales Dashboard

Your dashboard displays:
- **Pipeline Overview** - Current opportunities
- **Today's Tasks** - Follow-ups and calls
- **Recent Activities** - Customer interactions
- **Sales Metrics** - Your performance
- **Hot Leads** - High-priority prospects
- **This Month's Progress** - Revenue tracking

## CRM Management

### Managing Leads

#### Creating a New Lead
1. Navigate to CRM → Leads
2. Click "Add Lead"
3. Enter:
   - Company name
   - Contact person
   - Email and phone
   - Lead source
   - Initial interest
   - Budget range
4. Assign to yourself or team member
5. Click "Create Lead"

#### Lead Qualification
Score leads based on:
- **Budget** - Can they afford our products?
- **Authority** - Decision-making power?
- **Need** - Do they need our solutions?
- **Timeline** - When are they buying?

#### Converting Leads
When lead is qualified:
1. Go to lead details
2. Click "Convert to Contact"
3. Creates:
   - Contact record
   - Company record
   - Initial opportunity
4. Begin sales process

### Managing Contacts

#### Contact Information
Track:
- Basic details (name, title, email, phone)
- Company information
- Preferred communication method
- Relationship strength
- Interaction history
- Notes and tags

#### Adding Interactions
1. Go to contact record
2. Click "Log Activity"
3. Select type:
   - Phone call
   - Email
   - Meeting
   - Demo
   - Other
4. Add notes
5. Schedule follow-up
6. Save activity

### Managing Opportunities

#### Creating Opportunities
1. Navigate to CRM → Opportunities
2. Click "Create Opportunity"
3. Enter:
   - Opportunity name
   - Contact/Company
   - Estimated value
   - Expected close date
   - Stage
   - Probability
4. Add products of interest
5. Click "Create"

#### Opportunity Stages
1. **Qualification** - Initial discussion
2. **Needs Analysis** - Understanding requirements
3. **Proposal** - Quote provided
4. **Negotiation** - Terms discussion
5. **Closed Won** - Deal secured!
6. **Closed Lost** - Opportunity lost

#### Moving Through Pipeline
1. Go to opportunity details
2. Click "Change Stage"
3. Select new stage
4. Update probability
5. Add stage notes
6. Set next action
7. Save changes

## Creating Quotes

### Quote Process
1. Navigate to Orders → Create Quote
2. Select customer (or create new)
3. Add line items:
   - Product/material
   - Quantity
   - Unit price
   - Discount (if applicable)
4. Review totals
5. Add terms and conditions
6. Generate PDF quote
7. Send to customer

### Quote Follow-Up
Track quote status:
- **Sent** - Awaiting customer review
- **Viewed** - Customer opened quote
- **Questions** - Customer inquired
- **Negotiating** - Terms discussion
- **Accepted** - Ready to convert
- **Rejected** - Did not proceed

### Converting Quote to Order
When customer accepts:
1. Open quote
2. Click "Convert to Order"
3. Verify details
4. Add payment terms
5. Request deposit (if needed)
6. Submit order
7. Notify customer

## Order Management

### Creating Orders
1. Navigate to Orders → Create Order
2. Select customer
3. Add products:
   - From catalog
   - Custom items
   - Services
4. Set quantities and prices
5. Apply discounts
6. Add shipping details
7. Set payment terms
8. Submit order

### Order Details
Track:
- Order number
- Customer information
- Line items
- Pricing and discounts
- Production status
- Delivery timeline
- Payment status
- Special instructions

### Order Tracking
Monitor orders:
- Quote → Approved
- Approved → In Production
- In Production → QC
- QC → Shipped
- Shipped → Delivered

## Task Management

### Creating Tasks
1. Navigate to Tasks
2. Click "Create Task"
3. Enter:
   - Task description
   - Related to (lead/contact/opportunity)
   - Due date
   - Priority
   - Assigned to
4. Click "Create"

### Task Types
- **Follow-up Call** - Customer check-in
- **Send Quote** - Provide pricing
- **Schedule Demo** - Product presentation
- **Send Samples** - Material samples
- **Contract Review** - Terms discussion
- **Proposal Submission** - Formal proposal

### Managing Your Tasks
1. View in Tasks dashboard
2. Filter by:
   - Due date
   - Priority
   - Status
   - Related contact
3. Complete tasks:
   - Check off when done
   - Log outcome
   - Schedule next task
   - Add notes

## Best Practices

### Lead Management
- Follow up within 24 hours
- Qualify thoroughly before investing time
- Document all interactions
- Set clear next steps
- Keep information current

### Opportunity Management
- Update stages regularly
- Be realistic with probabilities
- Set accurate close dates
- Document objections and solutions
- Review pipeline weekly

### Customer Communication
- Respond promptly to inquiries
- Set clear expectations
- Follow through on commitments
- Keep customers informed
- Build long-term relationships

### Quote Accuracy
- Verify pricing before sending
- Include all costs and fees
- Clearly state terms
- Specify timelines
- Follow up systematically

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Create Order
**Solutions**:
- Verify customer exists in system
- Check all required fields filled
- Ensure products are active
- Verify pricing is current
- Contact admin if issue persists

### Quote Not Sending
**Solutions**:
- Check customer email is valid
- Verify quote is finalized
- Check spam/junk folder
- Try resending
- Download PDF and email manually

### Lost Opportunities
**Analyze**:
- Price too high?
- Timeline couldn't be met?
- Lost to competitor?
- Customer requirements changed?
- Document lessons learned

### Common Questions

**Q: How do I find a customer's past orders?**
A: Go to contact record → Orders tab to view full order history.

**Q: Can I modify a quote after sending?**
A: Yes, create a revised version. Previous versions are saved.

**Q: How do I request a custom product quote?**
A: Create opportunity, contact design team, they'll provide pricing.

**Q: What's the typical sales cycle?**
A: Varies by project: 2-8 weeks for standard items, 12+ weeks for custom projects.

**Q: How do I handle customer complaints?**
A: Document in CRM, involve customer success team, escalate to manager if needed.
