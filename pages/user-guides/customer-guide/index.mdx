# Customer Guide

Welcome to the Limn Systems Customer Portal - your hub for tracking orders, viewing documents, and managing your account.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Customer Portal
1. Navigate to app.limn.us.com/auth/customer
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Send Magic Link"
4. Check your email and click the link
5. You'll be logged in automatically

### First Time Login
- Your portal access is granted by a Limn administrator
- You'll receive an approval email with an "Access Portal" button
- Click the button to establish your session
- Future logins use the magic link process

## Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Order Summary** - Recent orders and their status
- **Pending Items** - Actions requiring your attention
- **Recent Documents** - Newly uploaded files
- **Invoice Status** - Outstanding payments
- **Shipment Tracking** - Active deliveries

## Managing Orders

### Viewing Your Orders
1. Navigate to Orders from the sidebar
2. View list of all your orders
3. Filter by:
   - Status (pending, in progress, completed)
   - Date range
   - Order number
4. Click on order to see details

### Order Details
Each order shows:
- Order number and date
- Line items and quantities
- Current status and timeline
- Assigned designer/project manager
- Estimated delivery date
- Special instructions

### Tracking Order Progress
Orders move through these stages:
1. **Quote** - Initial pricing proposal
2. **Approved** - Order confirmed and paid
3. **In Production** - Manufacturing in progress
4. **Quality Control** - QC inspection
5. **Shipped** - In transit to destination
6. **Delivered** - Order complete

### Downloading Order Documents
1. Go to order details
2. Scroll to "Documents" section
3. Click document name to download:
   - Order confirmation
   - Shop drawings
   - Shipping documents
   - Installation guides

## Document Management

### Accessing Documents
1. Navigate to Documents
2. View all project-related files
3. Organize by:
   - Order
   - Document type
   - Upload date
4. Download or preview files

### Document Types
- **Shop Drawings** - Manufacturing specifications
- **Installation Guides** - Setup instructions
- **Product Specs** - Material specifications
- **Certifications** - Quality certificates
- **Invoices** - Billing documents
- **Shipping Docs** - BOL, tracking info

## Financial Information

### Viewing Invoices
1. Navigate to Financials → Invoices
2. See all invoices with:
   - Invoice number and date
   - Amount due
   - Due date
   - Payment status
3. Click to view invoice details
4. Download PDF copies

### Payment History
View all payments:
- Payment date
- Amount paid
- Payment method
- Applied to which invoice

### Outstanding Balance
Dashboard shows:
- Total outstanding balance
- Overdue invoices (if any)
- Upcoming payment due dates
- Payment terms

## Shipping & Tracking

### Tracking Shipments
1. Navigate to Shipping
2. View active shipments
3. Click shipment for details:
   - Tracking number
   - Carrier information
   - Current location
   - Estimated delivery
   - Delivery address

### Shipment Notifications
Receive email alerts for:
- Shipment dispatched
- In transit updates
- Out for delivery
- Delivered confirmation

## Support

### Getting Help
- **Email**: support@limn.us.com
- **Response Time**: Within 24 hours (weekdays)
- **Urgent Issues**: Contact your account manager

### Common Questions

**Q: How do I place a new order?**
A: Contact your account manager or sales representative. They will create the order in the system.

**Q: Can I modify an order after approval?**
A: Contact your account manager. Some modifications may be possible depending on production status.

**Q: When will I receive my shipment?**
A: Check the order details page for estimated delivery date. Track shipment in real-time via Shipping section.

**Q: How do I download all documents for an order?**
A: Go to order details, scroll to Documents section, download individually or contact support for bulk download.

**Q: My magic link expired, what do I do?**
A: Request a new link from the login page. Links expire after 1 hour for security.
